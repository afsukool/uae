History of the UAE Flag

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) flag was designed in 1971 and symbolizes unity and patriotism. The colors of the flag represent Arab unity and stand for values of courage, honesty, love, and optimism. Each color has its own significance: the green represents prosperity, white stands for peace, black signifies strength, and red represents bravery.
