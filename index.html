History of the UAE Flag

The UAE flag was adopted on December 2, 1971, to represent the union of the emirates. The four colors of the flag represent Arab unity and are inspired by a line of poetry. The green symbolizes fertility, the white symbolizes peace, the black symbolizes the oil wealth, and the red represents courage and bravery.
